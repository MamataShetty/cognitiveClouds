Get Results With Our Methods

As a partner, we’re invested in you and your product so we guide you through our process while giving you our best ideas.

Process

Your product’s time-to-market is reduced by weeks because of our short development cycles and agile process.

Ship Products Faster

Experience faster results when you combine our proven process with cloud communication and collaboration tools. Retain us as a professional services partner if you wish to deviate from our process.

  • Discover

    Meet face-to-face with our product team and transfer your vision. We evaluate technologies and ask smart questions.

  • Plan

    We deliver a comprehensive project plan and technical architecture.

  • Prototype

    We deliver a functional prototype. You test-drive your product’s core features, so you make informed decisions.

  • Iterate

    We add and optimize all first version requirements while you receive weekly product builds.

  • Ship

    Since the code we write is always ready to go live, you decide when to launch.

  • Learn

    We record every customer interaction to optimize your conversion and retention rates. Use this data to prioritize next version enhancements.

  • Maintain

    Our operations team maintains and upgrades your infrastructure, monitoring your application 24/7.



Partner

Your success defines our success, so we partner with you and your stakeholders to invest in forming a long-term relationship.

Our partnerships often start with a trial engagement that we use to try each other out. Once the trial engagement is complete, we discuss ways in which we can partner on core product development, infrastructure engineering and mobile execution. Consider our team your extended team, helping you reach your product goals on time and within your budget.

Face-to-face communication is critical for you to transfer your passion and vision to the team building your product. If you’re building a large product, we recommend you fly to meet with our delivery team in India. When in-person meetings don’t make economic sense, we encourage face-to-face meetings through video conferencing tools.

Your best interests are in mind when we provide advice and strategic recommendations. In some cases, we’ve introduced our clients to Angel and VC investors in our network.


Co-Envision

Your passion drives your product vision, and we contribute our best ideas to help you build a better product; together, we co-envision your product.

With expertise spanning multiple decades and disciplines, our management team knows how to build successful products. The value you obtain through our partnership goes beyond pure execution because we guide you towards disruptive business models and innovative user acquisition strategies, which help you meet your business goals.

We suggest techniques and technologies for bringing your product to market faster while maintaining a robust and extensible architecture. A product idea is only as good as its execution so we work with you to make the best possible decisions around technical architecture and interaction design. Combine your domain expertise with the thoughts of our passionate innovators who strive to craft software products that exceed the expectations of your stakeholders, customers and you.


Collaborate

Your conversations guide our product team through the successful execution of your vision while providing transparency around the value we provide.

During the discovery process, your conversations with our team ensure we make the right assumptions around technical architecture and usability. When development begins, participate in daily scrums to provide input on deliverables and gain visibility into technical challenges. After delivery of your prototype, you obtain weekly product builds.

Build a connection with every member of our team, so you anticipate questions before they are asked. You control the pace of iteration and implementation because you have direct access to our team when your feedback is required. Excellent communication ensures on-time completion of deliverables.

Your product manager guides you through our process while using cloud productivity tools like Basecamp for collaboration, Pivotal Tracker for scrum user stories, and GoToMeeting for video and audio communication.


Succeed

Your success is measured by how the product we build is received by your customers and stakeholders; when you meet your goals, we meet our goals.

Before we draw a single pixel or write a single line of code, we review your business goals and ensure we’ve crafted a plan to achieve them. Whether you have a hard deadline, are the first mover or are leapfrogging a competitor, we work to understand all the components that will make our partnership a successful partnership.

We love big ideas. We strive to work with founders and business leaders who have ambitions of creating new markets and defining new industries. When you aim to push the limits of technical and social possibilities, we get excited and we deliver our best work.

We’re open to sharing risks and rewards by entering into creative business relationships that incentivize success.
