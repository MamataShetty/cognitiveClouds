Leadership

Prasanna Gopinath

Founder & CEO

With over 20 years of experience in the technology industry, Prasanna has held engineering leadership positions at companies like Sybase, Oracle, Cisco and Flip Video. Before founding CognitiveClouds, he served as Chief Technology Officer of Sourcebits, a Sequoia backed mobile services company. Earlier in his career, Prasanna researched video encoding algorithms at IBM TJ Watson Research and he co-founded eJunction and FonSquare. Prasanna holds a Masters in Computer Science from University of Illinois and BTech in Computer Science from IIT, Kanpur.

Ben Obear

Director of Business Development

Ben is responsible for generating new business and maintaining customer relationships. Previously, Ben was a top sales performer at Sourcebits, earning new business by recommending solutions and mobile strategy to customers. He was the Global Operations Manager and the first non-engineering hire at CloudCrowd, a crowdsourcing marketplace. Before Cloud Crowd, Ben founded a social-note sharing startup. Ben has a B.S. in Business Management from Cornell University.

Sandeep Ravichandran
