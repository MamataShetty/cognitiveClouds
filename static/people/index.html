Vision

CognitiveClouds fundamentally changes how the sourcing industry works by providing transparent communications from a globally distributed team of innovators, thought leaders, technical masterminds and product visionaries. Our process and tools ensure we meet your budgetary and time constraints while delivering the right product to your stakeholders and customers.

We craft smart applications for your smart organization. From enterprise grade cloud applications to remarkable mobile apps, we make intelligent software products your customers love. As a partner, our success depends upon your success, so we share our best ideas with you to ensure you meet your business goals.

For the past couple decades, our core team executed and managed products like yours at companies including IBM Watson Research, Oracle, Sybase, Cisco, Yahoo and Flip Video. We are a scrappy startup team, but our network is hundreds strong, allowing us to scale to deliver any product no matter the scope or deadline.

We employ an agile product development methodology to craft robust software products. Products we build are always production-ready, which means you can base your decision to go live on business factors and not technical ones. With a relentless focus on application speed, bug-free code, and offline optimization, we use modern technologies to engineer cloud back-ends, responsive websites and mobile applications.

New products and ideas keep our team excited and are the lifeblood of our company. We’re looking forward to learning more about how we can help you innovate.


Culture

Our open culture drives our success, attracting and retaining the most sought after people and clients because great company culture delivers on promises of innovation.

CognitiveClouds is built on an open culture foundation where opinion and feedback has equal value regardless of source, title or seniority. A no-nonsense company, our community of brilliant thinkers and doers value each other’s thoughts, which drive the products and processes of tomorrow.

Improvements to process roll out upon validation and we place equal value on ideas from new assistants to seasoned engineers because we believe working smarter gives us time to innovate and do our best work. Regular company updates focus on transparent communication about what’s happening, where we’re headed and why.

We bring the San Francisco software startup culture to India and abroad by allowing small talented teams to accomplish big things. While we have creative office space and free lunch, we don’t define culture that way. Culture is about positive interactions and providing the right environment to allow talented people to grow, pushing the limits of interaction design and technology.
