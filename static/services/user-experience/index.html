Your product’s utility and stickiness is driven by an intuitive user experience that balances simplicity with powerful discoverable features.. Our design process was created with education in mind. Our clients stick by our side through each phase, staying informed, providing feedback and learning how culture plays a major factor.

  • Define

    Definition starts with conducting research. Evaluating service strategies and conducting goal-mapping helps us learn about specific business goals. We get educated on the needs of the user by holding interviews, reviewing user profiles and establishing mental models. All of these key elements are how we identify opportunities surrounding a product blueprint.

  • Ideate

    The driving force behind Ideation is the power of brainstorming and creating concepts. We sketch concepts and test here. We create touch points, functions and use cases to gain insight into the stories we're trying to create. We use tools such as Influence and Verify to collect feedback as well as validate our ideas.

  • Prototype

    When considering interaction design, prototyping is where ideas are validated with feedback and quick iteration. Hi-fi wireframes, mockups and/or screenshots are then used to create design prototypes. By creating these screen or HTML prototypes with applications like Solidify, we're given the opportunity to extensively test how these design elements are viewed and interacted with from a user perspective.

  • Build

    Once iteration has been completed, we start to build. Design layouts and visual designs come together. We use a creative overview, moodboards and visualized concepts. Finally, we implement with our responsive framework, Foundation, our responsive framework. The result is a product built on collaborative thinking, user feedback and quick iteration.

  • Analyze

    Product research is a great way to gain this sort of insight. User feedback plays an important role by way of user testing partnered with the understanding of customer surveys, emails and interviews. Bug reporting and overall web audits, two of the most important elements of analyzing, are completed with the incorporation of our app, Notable. The faster we identify a problem, the quicker we can solve it.
